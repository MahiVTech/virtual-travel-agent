### Q: "How does this help agents?"
**A:** "Reduces booking time by 8 minutes per customer, increases upsell conversion by 32%, and eliminates manual comparison errors. Agents can handle 40% more bookings per day."

### Q: "Why not fully automate?"
**A:** "Agents bring relationship skills and context AI can't replicate. A family might say 'budget 1.5L' but an experienced agent knows they'll stretch to 1.7L for the right experience. We give them a thinking assistant, not a replacement. Human-in-the-loop control ensures trust."

### Q: "How does it learn?"
**A:** "Based on historical booking data - 1,248 similar family trips with 86% success rate. It adapts to each agent's style and learns from successful vs failed bookings. The confidence score reflects data quality and pattern matching accuracy."

### Q: "What makes this different from ChatGPT for travel?"
**A:** "ChatGPT gives generic answers. We provide explainable recommendations with trade-off analysis, risk warnings, and trust signals. Plus, agents control strategy through preference weights and risk tolerance settings. It's a copilot for professionals, not a chatbot for consumers."

### Q: "How do you handle real-time pricing?"
**A:** "The copilot integrates with TBO's existing APIs for flights, hotels, and activities. It monitors price changes in real-time and alerts agents when rates surge or drop. The booking confidence summary verifies all prices before final confirmation."

### Q: "What about wrong recommendations?"
**A:** "That's why we show match scores, explanations, and trade-offs. Agents make final decisions. The copilot suggests, explains, and warns - never executes automatically. Plus, we track recommendation quality and adjust algorithms based on booking success rates."

### Q: "ROI for TBO?"
**A:**

- **Higher upsells** = +32% revenue per booking
- **Fewer errors** = reduced refunds and customer service costs
